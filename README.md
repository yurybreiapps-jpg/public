Privacy Policy for MatchScore

Effective date: March 26, 2026

MatchScore is a scorekeeping application designed for sports such as tennis, pickleball, and ping-pong. This Privacy Policy explains how MatchScore handles user and device data.

Information We Collect
MatchScore does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information to the developer or third parties, except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Match Data
MatchScore may store match-related information locally on your device, including player names, sport type, match dates, scores, and match history, in order to provide the core functionality of the app.

Device Permissions
MatchScore only requests permissions that are necessary for app functionality. If additional permissions are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Services
MatchScore does not use third-party analytics, advertising, cloud storage, or tracking services in its current version.

Data Sharing
MatchScore does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion
Data stored by MatchScore remains on your device unless you delete it within the app, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
