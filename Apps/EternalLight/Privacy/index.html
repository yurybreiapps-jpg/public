Eternal Light Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

Eternal Light is a Christian prayer and reflection app designed to provide a calm, respectful, and privacy-conscious experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may access, store, or process, how that information is used, and what choices you have.

Eternal Light is designed to be local-first wherever practical. Personal reflections, preferences, and prayer history are intended to remain on your device by default.

1. Information the app stores on your device

Eternal Light may store the following information locally on your device so the app can function properly and remember your preferences:

  • language preference
  • denominational preference
  • prayer tone preference
  • audio and voice playback settings
  • reminder settings and preferred reminder time
  • quiet prayer duration preference
  • journal entries and reflection notes
  • prayer progress, streaks, goals, and related summary data
  • recent prayer snapshots and local prayer history used to reduce repetition
  • a cached copy of the most recent AI-generated prayer, when available, to improve continuity if live generation is unavailable

This information is generally stored on the device using local app storage. It is not intended to be shared with other users.

2. AI-generated prayer content

Eternal Light may offer AI-assisted prayer generation. When this feature is used, the app may send limited prayer-request information to a remote service in order to generate prayer text.

This may include information such as:

  • selected prayer circumstance or topic
  • language selection
  • denominational preference
  • prayer tone preference
  • quiet prayer duration target
  • the verse reference and verse text selected for the prayer
  • recent prayer titles or opening lines, when used to reduce repetition

The app does not ask you to enter highly sensitive personal information for normal use. Please do not submit private medical, financial, or other highly sensitive personal information in prayer requests or reflections.

If live AI generation is unavailable, the app may fall back to an on-device prayer experience or a previously cached prayer.

3. Notifications and reminders

If you choose to enable prayer reminders, Eternal Light may request permission to post notifications on your device. These reminders are used to deliver local daily prayer notifications you have chosen to receive.

You can disable reminders at any time in the app settings or in your device settings.

4. Audio features

Eternal Light includes spoken prayer playback features. These features are intended to read prayer content aloud using device or platform speech capabilities.

The current version is not intended to require microphone access for normal prayer playback. If a future version introduces voice input or recording, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Account and sign-in features

The current user experience is designed to work without requiring account creation for ordinary prayer use. If sign-in or connected account features are enabled in a future or optional flow, basic authentication information such as a user identifier, name, email address, login method, and session token may be processed and securely stored as needed to support that feature.

6. Information shared with third parties

Eternal Light does not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only as needed to operate the features you use, such as remote prayer generation or authentication services if those features are enabled.

7. Ads and analytics

Eternal Light is not intended to use in-app advertising in its current version. It is also not intended to include third-party analytics for ordinary prayer use unless clearly disclosed in a future update.

If advertising or analytics are added later, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or when those features are introduced.

8. Data retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you edit it, delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Remote service providers may temporarily process request data needed to generate responses or support service operation, subject to their own retention and operational practices.

9. Your choices

  • You may choose whether to enable notifications.
  • You may edit or delete journal entries inside the app.
  • You may clear app data by uninstalling the app or using your device settings, subject to platform behavior.
  • You may avoid optional connected features if you prefer a more local-only experience.

10. Children’s privacy

Eternal Light is not directed specifically to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app inappropriately, please contact the developer.

11. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to reduce unnecessary data collection and to keep locally stored information private on your device. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or feature updates. The updated version will be posted at the same public Privacy Policy URL, with a revised effective date.
