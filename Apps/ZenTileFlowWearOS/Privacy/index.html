Privacy Policy for ZEN Tile Flow

Effective date: May 15, 2026

ZEN Tile Flow is a standalone Wear OS puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information, puzzle progress, settings, purchase status, and device data.

1. Information We Collect

ZEN Tile Flow does not collect, transmit, store on external servers, sell, rent, or share personal information with the developer or third parties, except as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Game Progress and Settings

The app may store game-related information locally on your Wear OS device to provide puzzle gameplay and continue your progress. This information may include:

  • Current level and puzzle state
  • Tile positions and rotations
  • Total solved flows and trial progress
  • Sound on/off preference
  • Premium unlock status
  • App settings and preferences

This data is stored locally on your watch and is not transmitted to the developer in the current version of the app.

3. Music, Sound, and Haptics

ZEN Tile Flow may play local background music and sound effects and may use your device's vibration features for haptic feedback. These features operate locally on your device and do not send audio, gameplay, or haptic data to the developer.

4. Google Play Billing

ZEN Tile Flow may use Google Play Billing to offer a one-time premium unlock. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. The app may store your premium unlock status locally so the unlocked experience remains available after purchase.

The developer does not receive or store your payment card details. Google may process purchase-related information according to Google's own policies.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request device permissions that are reasonably necessary for app functionality, such as using vibration for haptic feedback, keeping the screen awake during play, and accessing Google Play services for billing.

If additional permissions are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

6. Third-Party Services

ZEN Tile Flow does not use third-party analytics, advertising, cloud storage, tracking, or profiling services in its current version.

Google Play Billing may be used for purchases, and Google Play may provide app distribution, purchase, update, and device compatibility services.

7. Data Sharing

ZEN Tile Flow does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share personal data with third parties.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored by the app remains on your Wear OS device unless you reset app settings, clear the app's storage through device settings, or uninstall the app.

Because this data is stored locally on your device, the developer generally does not have access to it and cannot delete it on your behalf.

9. Backup and Device Transfer

Depending on your device and Google account settings, Wear OS or Android backup and device transfer features may preserve local app progress and settings. These backup and transfer features are controlled by your device and Google account settings, not by the developer.

10. Data Security

ZEN Tile Flow is designed to keep game progress and settings stored locally on your device. While reasonable efforts are made to support secure app behavior, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

11. Children's Privacy

ZEN Tile Flow is not directed to children under the age of 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. International Users

If you use ZEN Tile Flow outside your country of residence, please note that data stored by the app remains on your device in the current version and is not transferred to the developer's servers.

13. Your Choices

You may reset game progress within the app, turn sound on or off in app settings, clear locally stored data through your Wear OS device settings, or uninstall the app at any time.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of ZEN Tile Flow after any updates indicates your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
