Privacy Policy for Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer

Effective date: May 8, 2026

Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer is a standalone Wear OS scorekeeping application for tennis, pickleball, and ping-pong. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information, match data, settings, and device data.

1. Information We Collect

Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer does not collect, transmit, store on external servers, sell, rent, or share personal information with the developer or third parties, except as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Match Data and Settings

The app may store match-related information locally on your Wear OS device to provide scorekeeping functionality. This information may include:

  • Selected sport
  • Scores and match state
  • Completed games or sets
  • Match timer information
  • Serving selection
  • Undo and redo state
  • App settings and preferences

This data is stored locally on your watch and is not transmitted to the developer or to any third party in the current version of the app.

3. Voice Announcements and Haptics

Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer may use your device's built-in text-to-speech and vibration features to provide voice announcements and haptic feedback. These features operate locally on your device and do not send voice, audio, or score data to the developer.

4. Device Permissions

The app may request device permissions that are reasonably necessary for app functionality, such as keeping the screen awake during scoring or using vibration for haptic feedback.

If additional permissions are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

5. Third-Party Services

Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer does not use third-party analytics, advertising, cloud storage, tracking, or profiling services in its current version.

If Google Play Billing or other third-party services are added for premium features in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe those services and how they handle data.

6. Data Sharing

Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share personal data with third parties.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored by the app remains on your Wear OS device unless you reset app settings, clear the app's storage through device settings, or uninstall the app.

Because this data is stored locally on your device, the developer generally does not have access to it and cannot delete it on your behalf.

8. Data Security

Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer is designed to keep match data and settings stored locally on your device. While reasonable efforts are made to support secure app behavior, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Children's Privacy

Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer is not directed to children under the age of 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Users

If you use Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer outside your country of residence, please note that data stored by the app remains on your device in the current version and is not transferred to the developer's servers.

11. Your Choices

You may reset app settings within the app, clear locally stored data through your Wear OS device settings, or uninstall the app at any time.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of Tennis Pickle Ping Scorer after any updates indicates your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
