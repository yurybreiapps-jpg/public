Privacy Policy for MatchScore

Effective date: March 26, 2026

MatchScore is a scorekeeping application designed for sports such as tennis, pickleball, and ping-pong. This Privacy Policy explains how MatchScore handles user information and device data.

1. Information We Collect

MatchScore does not collect, transmit, store on external servers, or sell personal information to the developer or third parties, except as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Match Data

MatchScore may store match-related information locally on your device in order to provide the core functionality of the app. This information may include:

  • Player names
  • Sport type
  • Match dates
  • Scores
  • Match history
  • App settings and preferences

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third party in the current version of the app.

3. Device Permissions

MatchScore only requests permissions that are reasonably necessary for app functionality. The specific permissions requested may depend on the features available in the version of the app you are using.

If additional permissions are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

4. Third-Party Services

MatchScore does not use third-party analytics, advertising, cloud storage, tracking, or profiling services in its current version.

If third-party services are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe the nature of those services and how they handle data.

5. Data Sharing

MatchScore does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share personal data with third parties.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored by MatchScore remains on your device unless and until you choose to delete it within the app, clear the app's storage through your device settings, or uninstall the app.

Because the data is stored locally on your device, the developer generally does not have access to it and cannot delete it on your behalf.

7. Data Security

MatchScore is designed to keep your data stored locally on your device. While reasonable efforts are made to support secure app behavior, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Children's Privacy

MatchScore is not directed to children under the age of 3, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 3.

Since MatchScore does not collect personal information in its current version, the developer does not knowingly process such data from children.

9. International Users

If you use MatchScore outside your country of residence, please note that data stored by the app remains on your device in the current version and is not transferred to the developer's servers.

10. Your Choices

You may choose what information to enter into the app, such as player names and match details. You may also remove locally stored data by deleting entries within the app, clearing the app's storage, or uninstalling the app.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of MatchScore after any updates indicates your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
